About Me

Mary Cancellieri is a full-stack web developer based in North Carolina. She was introduced to digital design through her minor in Professional Writing and continued her study in web development at UNC Charlotte School of Professional Studies. At UNC Charlotte, she completed a full-time 12 week bootcamp in web development in which she learned JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. She also gained experience in Progressive Web Applications(PWAs), MySQL, Sequelize, Application Programming Interfaces(APIs), and the MERN Stack.
